# Vision

We build systems that are:

- Reliable by default
- Secure by design
- Modular and evolvable
- Observable and debuggable
- Simple before being clever

The goal is not just to ship features.
The goal is to ship systems that can be maintained, extended, and trusted.

Every feature must:
- Provide clear user value
- Be consistent with platform patterns
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Preserve long-term maintainability

We optimize for:
- Clarity over magic
- Explicitness over hidden behavior
- Reusability over duplication
- Stability over short-term speed
